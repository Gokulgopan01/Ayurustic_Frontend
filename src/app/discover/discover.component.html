Discover the Craft

Botanical Silk

The Art of Botanical Silk

At Ayurustic, craftsmanship begins with material integrity. Our Ayurvastra-inspired silk is created through a considered process that unites fine mulberry silk with plant-based colour traditions. Each piece reflects patience, precision, and respect for heritage techniques adapted for modern living.

⸻
Pure Mulberry Silk

100% Mulberry Silk

We work exclusively with 100% mulberry silk, known for its long, uniform fibres and naturally smooth surface. The result is a fabric that feels lightweight, breathable, and refined against the skin.

Its natural sheen and fluid drape give each garment quiet elegance — a balance of comfort and understated luxury.

⸻
Botanical Colour

Botanical Colour, Thoughtfully Applied

Our silk is coloured using plant-based dyeing traditions inspired by time-honoured herbal knowledge. Instead of conventional chemical dyes, botanical formulations are carefully prepared and applied to the fabric through a slow, controlled process.

This method allows the silk to absorb colour gradually, creating rich yet nuanced tones. Subtle variations may occur — a natural mark of authenticity that reflects the character of botanical dyeing.

No two pieces are exactly alike. Each carries its own quiet individuality.

⸻
Time & Skill

Time, Skill, and Attention

True craftsmanship cannot be rushed. From fabric preparation to finishing, every stage requires skill and care.

Our production approach values attention to detail, material respect, and consistency in quality. The result is silk that not only looks refined but feels considered in every thread.

⸻
Crafted with Respect

Crafted with Respect

We believe luxury should be mindful. Our process prioritises natural fibres and plant-based colour applications, reducing reliance on conventional synthetic dyes while honouring traditional inspiration.

It is a modern expression of heritage — thoughtful, responsible, and quietly distinctive.

Herbal Wisdom

Herbal Wisdom in Every Thread

Ayurustic is rooted in tradition yet designed for today. By bringing together fine mulberry silk and botanical colour practices, we create pieces that feel both timeless and contemporary.

This is silk shaped by heritage — refined for modern living.
